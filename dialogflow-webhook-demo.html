Dialogflow Chatbot with NodeJS Webhook Integration

Here is a better interface with more functionalities offered in this chatbot. Especially the satellite imaging function.

Prompt for satellite: scan [country/city] (or other variations of the prompt)


List of intents

  • Help - "I am trapped", "Help!" etc
  • Hurricane speed - "How strong is the hurricane?" etc
  • Need medical - "I need medical" etc
  • Welcome - Welcome message
  • Headcount - "I would like to report a headcount" etc
  • Scan - "satellite image for texas" etc

Click on the agent on the bottom right to start
